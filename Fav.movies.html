Favourite Movies

1. Money Heists

"Money Heist" (La Casa de Papel) is a popular Spanish crime drama series centered around two elaborate heists, one at the Royal Mint of Spain and another at the Bank of Spain, orchestrated by a mastermind known as "The Professor" and executed by a diverse group of criminals who use city names as aliases. Reviews generally praise its gripping storyline, complex characters, and the Professor's calculated strategies, though some critics note issues with pacing, unrealistic plot points, and occasional melodrama, with many recommending it for its entertainment value despite its flaws.

2. Stranger Things

"Stranger Things" is a Netflix sci-fi horror series set in the 1980s, where a group of kids in Hawkins, Indiana, uncover supernatural mysteries, secret government experiments, and a terrifying alternate dimension called the Upside Down after a boy vanishes.

Stranger Things (Trailer)

3. Attack of Titan

"Attack on Titan" is a Japanese anime series set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight against the Titans and uncover the truth behind their existence. The series is known for its intense action, complex characters, and deep themes of freedom, sacrifice, and the human condition.

4. Demon Slayer

"The Infinity Castle is an extra-dimensional fortress in "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba" that serves as the primary base for Muzan Kibutsuji and the Upper Rank Demons, where key battles of the series unfold.
